# Context
Salonomi is a B2B booking and management platform for small businesses in the health and beauty sector in Latin America.

## The Problem
The original platform was not mobile-friendly. Analytics showed that more than 80% of customers used the tool from mobile devices, finding great difficulties in recording sales and managing commissions.

## Design Process
### Audit
Review of all application screens to identify critical usability issues.

### Prioritization
Redesign of 7 key screens, including invoice management, balancing usability with ease of technical implementation for the development team.

## Final Solution
A fully responsive web-app with improved information hierarchy. Critical functions such as quick edit buttons and simplified invoice generation were added.
