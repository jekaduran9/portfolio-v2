## 🚀 The Challenge: From a delicious product to a vibrant brand
Tropi Yummy landed in Barcelona with a clear dream: to bring the authentic taste of the tropics to a cosmopolitan city. The client had star products —like mango biche and obleas— but lacked a visual identity.

My challenge was to build a brand from scratch that would convey freshness, joy, and modern "Latinity," avoiding clichés and ensuring consistent communication in both digital and physical environments.

## 🧠 Strategy and Concept: More than fruit, a Mood
Before drawing the first line, I defined the brand's visual ecosystem based on:

*   **Market Research:** Analysis of local competition in Barcelona and street food trends.
*   **Target Audience:** Youth, families, and tourists looking for a healthy and exotic alternative.
*   **The DNA:** Tropi Yummy doesn't just sell snacks; it sells a "mini-trip to the tropics."

## 🎨 Visual Identity: The Taste of Graphics
The logo was designed to be the ambassador of that tropical energy, prioritizing legibility and freshness.

*   **Typography:** I used rounded, friendly fonts that convey closeness and softness.
*   **Dynamism:** The design integrates subtle organic movement that evokes the fluidity of natural juices and Caribbean vitality.
*   **Color Palette:** A vibrant palette (mango, lime, papaya, and guava) that awakens the appetite and distinguishes the brand in the saturated urban environment.

## 📸 Art Direction and Production: The Digital and Hybrid Menu
For the menu, I developed an art direction where the product is the absolute protagonist.

### 🤖 AI Innovation
Faced with an initial lack of photographic material, I used Artificial Intelligence to generate high-fidelity visuals. By maintaining the brand's palette and realistic textures, we managed to launch a complete and professional menu even before the physical opening.

### 📸 Real Optimization
After the opening, I integrated real photography of the shop. I performed an exhaustive post-production process (color correction, lighting, and background cleaning) so that the real photos could coexist in harmony with those generated by AI.

## ✨ Result
A hybrid, coherent, and professional aesthetic that allows Tropi Yummy to compete visually from day one, achieving impactful communication both at the point of sale and on digital platforms.
