# Context
Tropi Yummy arrived in Barcelona with authentic products (mango biche, fruit salads, obleas), but completely lacked a brand identity. There was no logo, color palette, or defined visual style. The challenge was to build a brand from scratch that conveyed tropical freshness and joy.

## The Problem
The lack of identity prevented connecting with a diverse audience in a competitive urban environment like Barcelona. The business needed a professional image to communicate the quality of its natural products.

## Design Process
### Research
Immersion in the tropical universe and analysis of modern Caribbean aesthetics to define a refreshing mood that avoided common clichés.

### Visual Identity
Development of a logo with rounded typography that evokes closeness. The vibrant palette was directly inspired by fruits: mango, lime, papaya, and guava.

### Art Direction
Creation of the menu using a hybrid strategy: high-quality AI image generation for initial products and professional editing of real photographs after the opening.

## Final Solution
A coherent and energy-filled visual identity was achieved, successfully applied to the physical menu and social media, facilitating the successful launch of the brand in the local market.
