# Context
Findasense sought to renew its digital presence to reflect its evolution as a strategic consultancy.

## The Problem
The previous website did not correctly communicate the value of its current services or its global reach.

## Design Process
We conducted stakeholder interviews, content auditing, and redesigned the information architecture to improve navigation.

## Final Solution
A clean, modular, and easy-to-navigate platform that highlights the agency's portfolio.
