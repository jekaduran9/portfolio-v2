# Context
Findasense was looking to renew its digital presence to reflect its evolution as a strategic consultancy.

## Problem
The navigation was confusing and did not effectively communicate their core services. The main goal was to improve the conversion rate on the service pages.

## Design Process
We conducted stakeholder interviews and an exhaustive content audit. We defined a new information architecture based on user insights.

### Final Solution
A clean website with a powerful visual narrative that highlights the agency's success stories.
