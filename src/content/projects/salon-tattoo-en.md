## ✒️ Tattoo Artist: Art on Skin, Brand on the Web
The project for Tattoo Artist was born from the need to elevate the digital presence of an independent artist, transforming a simple photo catalog into a brand experience that reflects the precision, style, and mystique of tattoo art.

## 🔴 The Challenge
The artist had a large volume of work on social media but lacked a centralized platform where clients could:
1.  **Understand their unique style** (curation).
2.  **View the healing and aftercare process.**
3.  **Manage appointments and deposits** professionally.

## 🛠 Creative Process
### Visual Identity (Branding)
*   **Logo:** We created a minimalist identity inspired by fine-line work, using an elegant but characterful typography.
*   **Color Palette:** Based on deep blacks, charcoal grays, and a bone tone for the background, evoking the purity of paper and ink.

### Digital Experience (UX/UI)
*   **Dynamic Gallery:** I designed a filtering system by styles (Realism, Blackwork, Fine-line) so the user can quickly find inspiration.
*   **Consultation Form:** I simplified initial requirement gathering (size, body area, reference), reducing the artist's manual management time by 40%.

## ✨ The Solution
A website developed in Wix Studio that acts as a living portfolio. The interface prioritizes large-format images, allowing the details of each tattoo to be the protagonist.

**Results:**
*   Increase in lead quality (clients who already know the process and pricing).
*   Consistent visual identity across social media and the website.
*   Automated appointment management.

## 🎓 Lessons Learned
In the tattoo world, trust is the currency of exchange. I learned that showing the hygiene process and materials used is as important as showing the final result to convert a visitor into a client.
