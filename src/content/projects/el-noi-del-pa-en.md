# Context
El Noi del Pa is a 100% gluten-free artisanal bakery in Barcelona that sought to transfer its essence of warmth and closeness to the digital environment.

## The Problem
They needed a professional online presence that allowed users to explore their products and place orders easily, with a platform manageable by the client themselves.

## Design Process
### Research
Competitor audit and content/catalog organization in Notion to ensure a clear structure from the start.

### Design & Implementation
Creation of a Figma interface focused on accessibility. The final implementation was done in Wix, configuring a complete and easy-to-update e-commerce system.

## Final Solution
A visually warm website that includes:
*   **Storytelling:** "Our Story" section to emotionally connect with the celiac community.
*   **Organized Catalog:** Clear division by categories (bakery, pastries, cakes).
*   **E-commerce & Logistics:** Payment gateway integration and direct order system via Glovo to expand local reach.
