# Context
Grip is a Dutch startup that offers at-home blood tests to provide information on hormonal health and fertility.

## The Problem
The original site used a generic template. With growth, there was a need to professionalize the experience. 90% of users accessed from mobile devices, but the site was not optimized for it, affecting conversion.

## Design Process
### Research
Research and client interviews to understand their concerns about hormonal health. Constant monitoring with Hotjar to detect bottlenecks in the purchase flow.

### Mobile-First Design
Redesign of 80% of the pages focusing the experience on mobile devices, extending the design system with new modular components.

## Final Solution
An optimized purchase flow and a coherent visual identity that conveys medical trust and closeness.

**Results:**
*   **10% monthly growth** in sales.
*   Conversion rate maintained above 1% after the full redesign.
